MIRISK PEER REVIEW

Thank you for agreeing to Peer Review MIRISK. To complete this Peer Review, we suggest:

  • At the end of this preamble, you stop, and go to MIRISK
  • At MIRISK, take the tutorial and then try MIRISK on one or several projects in various regions you are familiar with
  • Then, come back to this Peer Review form, and complete the table below. While answering the questions, you may of course find it useful to return to MIRISK - if so, please do so.
  • Please only complete this Feedback form once. However, you can provide comments at any time, via the MIRISK Wiki facility, which is accessed from the MAIN page.

Introduction: As has been emphasized, MIRISK is in Beta version, so that some data and other aspects are not finalized. However, the general framework is complete. Version 1.0 has been constructed using only HOTSPOTS data, and it is fully recognized that more detailed data should be developed in subsequent versions.

Instructions: To facilitate your Peer Review, we ask the following specific questions. Of course, following these questions, you are invited to provide any other comments you wish to make.

In the following Table, for each numbered question, please Score the feature 0-10, where:

0 = Totally Terrible or 100% No, and needs complete Revision, and
10 = Excellent or 100% Yes, and needs No Revision.

Space is provided for you to elaborate and comment as to why a feature may score less than 10, and how it might be revised. For each question, feel free in the Comment section to elaborate.
